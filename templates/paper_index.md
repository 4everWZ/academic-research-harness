## Role definitions

- `baseline`: candidate method for empirical comparison.
- `competing_method`: related approach that overlaps with the proposed direction.
- `supporting_mechanism`: supports a mechanism, design rationale, or architectural choice.
- `background`: provides task/problem context.
- `dataset_metric_reference`: supports dataset, metric, or benchmark setup.
- `implementation_reference`: useful implementation details or code reference.
- `weak_signal`: awareness only; not central evidence.

## Evidence strength definitions

- `strong`: directly relevant, transparent evaluation, strong baselines, low reproducibility risk.
- `medium`: relevant and useful but has some uncertainty.
- `weak`: partial relevance or high reproducibility/protocol risk.
- `reject`: do not use as evidence.
